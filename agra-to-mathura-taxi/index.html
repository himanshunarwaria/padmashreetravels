Places to Visit in Mathura

When you hire a taxi from Agra to Mathura, you get the freedom to explore the city’s rich spiritual heritage at your own pace. Our drivers cover all these must-visit spots:

Krishna Janmabhoomi

Lord Krishna’s birthplace — the most sacred site in Mathura. Our drivers drop you at the entrance and wait nearby while you complete darshan.

Dwarkadhish Temple

One of the oldest and most beautiful temples in Mathura, known for stunning architecture and vibrant festivals during Janmashtami and Holi.

Vishram Ghat

Sacred ghat on the Yamuna where Lord Krishna rested after defeating Kansa. The evening aarti ceremony is a must-see experience.

Mathura Museum

Home to fine collections of Kushan and Gupta period sculptures. A great stop for history and art lovers.

Kusum Sarovar

A beautiful historic reservoir near Govardhan, surrounded by stunning ghats and ideal for photography and peaceful walks.

Govardhan Hill

Sacred hill associated with Lord Krishna. Add this as an optional stop on your return journey from Mathura.
