Agra to Mathura Taxi – Visit Lord Krishna’s Birthplace, 58 km Away

The most popular day trip from Agra is the 58 km ride to Mathura along NH-2. Our Agra to Mathura taxi fare starts at ₹1,500 one way and ₹3,000 round trip in an AC sedan. Visit Krishna Janmabhoomi, the most sacred site in Mathura, then Dwarkadhish Temple and the peaceful Vishram Ghat on the Yamuna. The evening aarti at Vishram Ghat is a must-see experience. Most travellers combine Mathura with a visit to Vrindavan — our Mathura Vrindavan tour from Agra covers both cities in one full day for ₹3,000.

Agra to Vrindavan Cab – Temple Town on the Yamuna, 70 km Away

Vrindavan, 70–75 km from Agra via Mathura, is home to over 5,000 temples. Our Agra to Vrindavan cab starts at ₹1,800 one way. Top temples include Banke Bihari Temple, Prem Mandir, ISKCON Vrindavan, Radha Vallabh Temple, and Nidhivan. Drivers know the narrow temple lanes and wait patiently during darshan — you will never feel rushed. During Janmashtami and Holi, Vrindavan transforms into an extraordinary festival of lights and devotion. Book your cab a few days in advance during peak seasons.

Agra Local Sightseeing Taxi – Cover Every Monument in One Day

Agra has far more to offer than just the Taj Mahal. A full-day Agra local sightseeing taxi covers the Taj Mahal at sunrise, the grand Agra Fort just 2.5 km away, Baby Taj (Itimad-ud-Daulah) across the Yamuna, Akbar’s Tomb at Sikandra, and finally Mehtab Bagh for the best sunset view of the Taj reflected in the river. Our full-day Agra sightseeing package starts at ₹2,500 for an AC sedan (8 hours, 80 km). Your driver stores luggage safely in the car while you explore each monument.

Agra Airport Taxi Service – On-Time Pickups, Fixed Fare

Arriving at Agra’s Kheria Airport (AGR) or Agra Cantonment Railway Station? Our Agra airport taxi service guarantees a clean, on-time AC car at arrivals. Transfers start at ₹800 within the city. You can also combine airport pickup with a full-day sightseeing tour — one seamless trip, fixed price. Our driver picks you up at arrival, takes you through Agra’s monuments, and drops you at your hotel or station in the evening.
