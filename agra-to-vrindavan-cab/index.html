Top Temples to Visit in Vrindavan

Our Agra to Vrindavan cab takes you to all major temples. Your driver knows the best visiting order based on opening hours and crowd patterns:

🏭 Banke Bihari Temple

The most famous temple in Vrindavan, dedicated to Lord Krishna. Known for the unique “jhanki” darshan where the curtain is periodically drawn.

✨ Prem Mandir

Stunning white marble temple built by Jagadguru Kripalu Maharaj. The evening light show makes it an unforgettable experience.

🏛 ISKCON Temple

The grand Krishna Balaram Mandir founded by Srila Prabhupada. Beautiful architecture, peaceful atmosphere, and excellent prasadam.

🌿 Nidhivan

A mysterious sacred grove where, according to legend, Lord Krishna performs Raas Leela every night. No one is allowed after sunset.

🌊 Kesi Ghat & Yamuna

Sacred bathing ghat on the Yamuna river. The evening aarti ceremony at Kesi Ghat is a deeply spiritual experience.

🏛 Radha Vallabh Temple

One of the oldest temples in Vrindavan, dating to 1585. Known for its unique architectural style and deep devotional significance.
