Agra to Mathura & Vrindavan — One Day Trip Plan

Hour-by-hour itinerary for visiting both holy cities from Agra. Includes distance, taxi fares, temples to cover, food tips, and practical advice.

Padma Shree TravelsApril 202614 min read

Trip at a Glance

Route: Agra → Mathura → Vrindavan → Agra

Distance: 200–250 km round trip

Duration: 8–10 hours full day

Taxi Fare: ₹3,000 AC sedan round trip

Key Temples: Krishna Janmabhoomi, Banke Bihari, Prem Mandir

The Mathura Vrindavan one day trip from Agra is one of the most popular pilgrimage excursions in North India. Mathura, the birthplace of Lord Krishna, and Vrindavan, where he spent his childhood, are both within 60–75 km of Agra — making it a comfortable day trip by taxi.

Agra to Mathura Distance & Route

The distance from Agra to Mathura is approximately 58–62 km via NH-2. The drive takes about 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes. From Mathura, Vrindavan is just 15 km further (25–30 minutes).

Complete One Day Itinerary

1
6:30 AM — Pickup

Depart from Agra

Driver picks you up from hotel, station, or any Agra location. Start early to make the most of the day.

2
8:00 AM — Mathura

Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple

The birthplace of Lord Krishna. Visit the main temple and underground prison cell. Allow 1–1.5 hours.

3
9:30 AM

Dwarkadhish Temple

Beautiful temple with intricate architecture and vibrant atmosphere. 45 minutes recommended.

4
10:30 AM

Vishram Ghat

Sacred ghat where Lord Krishna rested after defeating Kansa. Quick 20-minute stop.

5
11:15 AM — Drive to Vrindavan

Arrive in Vrindavan

Short 25-minute drive. Driver navigates narrow lanes and finds parking.

6
11:45 AM

Banke Bihari Temple

Most famous temple in Vrindavan. Unique “jhanki” darshan. 45 minutes.

7
12:45 PM

Lunch Break

Vegetarian restaurants near temples. ISKCON prasadam is an excellent and affordable option.

8
1:45 PM

ISKCON Temple

Grand Krishna Balaram Mandir. Beautiful architecture, peaceful chanting. 45 minutes.

9
2:45 PM

Prem Mandir

Stunning white marble temple. Evening light show at sunset is spectacular.

10
4:00 PM

Nidhivan & Kesi Ghat

Sacred grove and Yamuna ghat. Stay for evening aarti if time permits.

11
5:00 PM

Return to Agra

Reach Agra by 6:30–7:00 PM. Drop at hotel, station, or any Agra location.

💡 Temple Timing Tip

Most temples close 12:00–4:00 PM. Plan morning visits carefully. Your taxi driver knows exact timings and will guide your schedule.

Why a Private Taxi Is Best

  • Door-to-door — Pickup from Agra hotel, drop back at same location.
  • Flexible — Spend more time at temples you love, skip others.
  • No navigation stress — Vrindavan’s lanes are confusing. Your driver knows every shortcut.
  • Safe storage — Leave bags and shoes in the car during temple visits.
  • AC comfort — Essential between temple visits, especially in warm weather.
