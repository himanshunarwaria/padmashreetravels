Agra Sightseeing in One Day — Complete Itinerary

From Taj Mahal at sunrise to Mehtab Bagh at sunset — cover every major monument in a single day. Hour-by-hour plan with entry fees and taxi fares.

Padma Shree TravelsApril 202612 min read

With an early start and a dedicated sightseeing taxi, you can see all major Agra monuments in one day. This itinerary has been refined from hundreds of actual trips our drivers complete monthly.

Hour-by-Hour Itinerary

🏭 6:00 AM — Taj Mahal (Sunrise)

⏱ 2.5 hours  ·  🎫 ₹50 Indian, ₹1,100 Foreign  ·  East Gate recommended

Start at the world’s most beautiful monument. Arrive before sunrise for the shortest queue. Watch the marble change colour as the sun rises over the Yamuna.

🏛 9:00 AM — Agra Fort

⏱ 1.5 hours  ·  🎫 ₹50 Indian, ₹650 Foreign  ·  2.5 km from Taj

Massive red sandstone fort with stunning palaces. See the Musamman Burj where Shah Jahan gazed at the Taj Mahal during his imprisonment.

🏭 11:00 AM — Baby Taj (Itimad-ud-Daulah)

⏱ 45 min  ·  🎫 ₹30 Indian, ₹310 Foreign

Exquisite marble tomb with pietra dura inlay work that inspired the Taj Mahal. Peaceful gardens, far fewer crowds than the main monuments.

🍴 12:00 PM — Lunch Break

⏱ 1 hour  ·  Driver recommends restaurants

Try Pinch of Spice or local options near the fort area. Don’t miss Agra’s famous Petha sweets from shops near Sadar Bazaar.

🏛 1:30 PM — Akbar’s Tomb, Sikandra

⏱ 1 hour  ·  🎫 ₹35 Indian, ₹310 Foreign  ·  13 km from centre

Grand mausoleum blending Hindu, Islamic, Christian, and Buddhist architectural styles. Deer and monkeys roam freely in the lush gardens.

🌊 3:30 PM — Mehtab Bagh (Sunset)

⏱ 45 min  ·  🎫 ₹30 Indian, ₹310 Foreign

The most stunning sunset view of the Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna. The photograph you will frame forever. Go 30 minutes before sunset for the best light.
